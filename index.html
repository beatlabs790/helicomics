The Mystery of Room 13

Tucked away in the sub-basement of the Helix Biotech facility, Room Number 13 housed experiments that went beyond traditional genetics. In the year 2026, researchers attempting to transmit digital code directly into biological cells triggered a spatial feedback loop.

Physical boundaries within the lab began to ripple. The walls, machinery, and research logs dissolved into glowing copper streams. Witness the psychological containment horror as Vance fights to shut down the cell sequence before it rewrites local reality.
